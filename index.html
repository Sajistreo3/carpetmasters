What We Do & Who We Are

Frequently Asked Questions

What cleaning methods do you use?

We use a variety of methods, including steam cleaning, dry cleaning, and hot water extraction, to ensure the best results for every type of carpet and upholstery.

How long does a typical carpet cleaning session take?

The duration of a carpet cleaning session can vary depending on the size of the area and the condition of the carpets. On average, it takes approximately 20 minutes per room, but heavily soiled areas or larger spaces may require more time.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Absolutely! Your safety and comfort are our top priorities. We use eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning products designed to be safe for pets, children, and the environment.

Can you clean carpets made from natural fibers?

Yes, we have specific cleaning processes tailored for natural fiber carpets such as wool, silk, and cotton. We use gentle, yet effective techniques to ensure that your natural fiber carpets are cleaned thoroughly without damaging the delicate material.

How long will it take for my carpets to dry after cleaning?

Drying times can vary based on the method used and the humidity and temperature in your home. Generally, it takes about 6-10 hours for carpets to dry completely after steam cleaning. We recommend keeping the room ventilated to speed up the drying process.

Can you remove all types of stains?

While we can remove most stains, the success rate depends on the type of stain, the length of time the stain has been on the carpet, and the carpet’s material. We can assess and treat stains such as wine, coffee, ink, and pet stains with specialized techniques for the best possible outcome.

What is the average cost of your carpet cleaning services?

Our pricing depends on the size of the area being cleaned and the type of cleaning required. We offer free estimates, and you can contact us to get a detailed quote based on your specific needs.

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned?

For most homes, we recommend professional cleaning at least once a year to maintain carpet hygiene and appearance. For high traffic areas or households with pets, children, or allergies, we recommend cleaning more frequently.
